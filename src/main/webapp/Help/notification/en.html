Title: Registration

Faculty of Social Sciences

Dean

21.01.2014

Registration for the spring semester of 2013-2014 academic years will be held by the following rules. Please, pay attention:

1. Registration will be held from January 27th to February 7th, 2014. Classes will start on February 3rd, 2014.

2. Students, who will not register until 17:00 on the 7th of February will pay an additional fee. The fee for unregistered students is the following: registration till 17:00 of February 14th is 500 soms, till 17:00 of February 21st - 750 soms, till 17:00 of February 28th -1000 soms.

3. Students, who will not register until 17:00 of February 28th won’t be able to pass registration later, they will have to an academic leave.

4. It is necessary for supervisors of the groups to inform problem students to pass registration on time.

Prof.Dr. Osman Gökalp
